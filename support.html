About Tome

Tome is a beautiful and powerful book library management app designed to help you organize, track, and explore your personal book collection. Whether you're managing a few favorites or an extensive library, Tome makes it easy to keep track of what you're reading, what you want to read, and what you've already finished.

Features

  • Comprehensive Book Database: Search and add books from extensive online catalogs
  • Reading Status Tracking: Organize books by Currently Reading, To Read, and Read
  • Smart Organization: Use tags and categories to organize your library your way
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Access your library on both iOS and macOS
  • Native Experience: Beautiful native interface designed specifically for Apple platforms
  • Manual Entry: Add books manually when they're not available in the database
  • Bulk Import: Quickly add multiple books to your library

Getting Started

Adding Your First Book

To add a book to your library, simply tap the "+" button and choose one of three options:

  • Search: Find books by title, author, or ISBN
  • Manual Entry: Enter book details manually
  • Bulk Import: Add multiple books at once

Organizing Your Library

Use the sidebar to navigate between different views of your library:

  • All Books: View your entire collection
  • Currently Reading: Track books you're actively reading
  • To Read: Your reading wishlist
  • Read: Books you've finished
  • Tags: Custom organization categories

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my library between devices?
Tome uses iCloud to automatically sync your library between all your devices. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices and have iCloud enabled for Tome.
Can I export my book collection?
Yes! You can export your library data from the Settings menu. This creates a backup of your collection that you can keep for your records.
Where does the book information come from?
Tome searches comprehensive online book databases including Open Library to provide accurate book information, cover images, and metadata.
What if I can't find a book in the search?
You can always add books manually! Use the "Manual Entry" option to input all the book details yourself, including the title, author, ISBN, and other information.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your library data is stored locally on your device and synced through your personal iCloud account. We don't collect, access, or share any of your data.
How do I delete a book from my library?
Select the book you want to remove and use the delete option in the book's detail view. On iOS, you can also swipe left on a book in the list view to delete it.

System Requirements

  • iOS: Requires iOS 17.0 or later
  • macOS: Requires macOS 14.0 or later
  • iCloud: Recommended for syncing between devices

Privacy Policy

Tome is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or data about your book library. All data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced through your personal iCloud account, which is protected by Apple's security measures.

When you search for books, queries are sent to third-party book database services (such as Open Library) to retrieve book information. These searches are anonymous and not associated with your identity.
